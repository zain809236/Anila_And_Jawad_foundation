Meethi Zindagi

"Our dedication to enhancing the lives of Pakistani individuals living with type 1 diabetes lies at the core of our mission. We are committed to ensuring that diabetes care is readily accessible to everyone with type 1 diabetes..."

Meethi Zindagi is dedicated to supporting children and people in Pakistan living with type 1 diabetes. They transform the lives of those that are unable to access the diabetes supplies needed, providing them with essential resources like insulin and testing. By showcasing your support and donations, you can truly change the lives of many across Pakistan.

The founder, Dr. Sanya Amjad, with her prestigious education in PhD Microchip engineering and senior positions in the International Diabetes Federation and her lived experience with diabetes, has created a movement that is transforming the lives of many by initiatives that are founded by excellent knowledge and expertise.

My Impact Meter

"We are creating a system in which individuals, communities, organizations and countries are motivated and incentivized to come together and create a prosperous and just world." My Impact Meter is revolutionizing the current landscape for giving charity. The innovative CEO & founder Kamal Ghaemian has designed My Impact Meter app to streamline the process of giving charity and it makes sure that the process is safer, more transparent and traceable.

By simply inputting the CNIC (National ID number) & phone number of the individual (impactee) you wish to donate to, you can directly donate an amount of essential supplies or services such as flour, bread, education, soap etc. from the impactor's account wallet. The impactee can access these supplies by going to the nearest grocery store, run through the payment system 'EasyPaisa'. The impactee can scan the goods they require from the Impact App, track stock, and receive the items they need.

The use of the CNIC & phone number eliminates any concerns of whether the funds are being received and utilized for the right purposes. It introduces focused charity directly provided in the form of selected ration (essential supplies) / service rather than monetary donation.

Legal Aid Society

"Connecting vulnerable and disempowered end-users of justice with effective and expedient services for the delivery of justice."

The Legal Aid Society was founded in 2013 by Justice (Rtd.) Nasir Aslam Zahid, a former Chief Justice of the Sindh High Court and former Supreme Court Judge of Pakistan. Justice Zahid, known for his commitment to human rights and social justice, initiated LAS to fill the huge gap in legal aid services in Pakistan, particularly for impoverished and underserved populations. The core purpose is to empower citizens by ensuring they understand their legal rights and have access to fair legal representation, especially those most at risk of exploitation, abuse, or discrimination.

The Society's work includes handling cases related to family law, criminal law, and civil disputes, with a focus on marginalized communities, women, and children. They also conduct legal awareness programs to educate the public about their rights and responsibilities. Through these efforts, the Legal Aid Society strives to promote a more equitable and just society in Pakistan.
