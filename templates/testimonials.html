JAFA Girls Soccer Team

Jafa is a recognised and awarded academy promoting football and girls empowerment. They received an award in May 2025 from the Director Sports of Habib Bank, to show recognition of Jafa's continuous efforts to uplift the youth ‚Äî especially young girls through sports.

"At JAFA, we believe in equal fields, equal dreams, and unstoppable passion. Proud to keep pushing boundaries!"

JAFA Football Academy has over 200 members and 8 teams which includes U10, U12, U14, U16, and Senior Team Girls based in Lyari, Pakistan which is also known as Mini Brazil.

They have been operating as a football academy for over 10 years, and have a great local supporter base, including players, parents, volunteers, administrators and coaches like Javed Arab who is a professional football player and coach. He played with international teams and he is a former player of Pakistan football team and Nasir Arab who played for famous and professional club of Oman known as Al-Seeb FC.
